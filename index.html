About Eric

A Lifetime of
Playing & Teaching

I first laid my hands on a piano at the age of 5, picked up my first guitar at age 14, and have been in love ever since.

Over the past 11 years of my professional career, I've had the privilege of teaching 300+ students - from curious beginners just holding a guitar for the first time, to seasoned players looking to break through a creative plateau, and strive for excellence.

My teaching philosophy is simple - Music should be about freedom and creative expression, not mindless repetition. As such, I spend a lot of time helping my students build genuine musicality alongside technique - I believe that understanding why a chord progression works, is just as important as being able to play it. Lessons are tailored to your goals, your pace, and most importantly around the music you are actually excited to play.

With over 10 years of experience in teaching and performing, I'm confident that I'll be able to help you pursue your musical goals. I look forward to hearing your voice on the instrument of your choice!

What I Teach

Lessons for Every Path

Each lesson is customized to your goals and musical interests. No cookie-cutter curriculum — just focused, meaningful progress.

Acoustic Guitar

From country to pop, rock to blues, come experience the joy of expressing yourself through guitar!

  • Proper posture and hand position
  • Finger dexterity and technique
  • Build a repertoire from timeless classics
  • Music theory fundamentals

Electric Guitar

Awaken the rockstar inside of you! Explore the electric guitar with focus on technique, tone and creative expression.

  • Technique and tone
  • Rhythm guitar playing
  • Improvisation and soloing
  • Effects and amp settings
  • Explore various musical styles (RnB, Blues, Rock etc.)

Piano

Great as a first instrument - the piano serves as a great foundation for whatever instrument you plan to explore next! Develop hand independence, sight-reading and a strong foundation in music theory.

  • Hand position and technique
  • How to read classical music notation
  • Classical and contemporary styles
  • Explore various scales, chord progressions and more!

Ukulele

A great choice for toddlers first picking up a stringed instrument - ukulele serves as a great stepping stone before picking up a guitar! Recommended for young children. (Ages 3+)

  • Proper posture and technique
  • Rhythmic basics
  • Left and right hand synchronization
  • Play songs from your favorite shows, musicals and more!

Music Theory

If your goal is to move beyond playing other people's songs, and start developing a voice of your own, music theory is exactly what you need! Develop an understanding of scales, chords and rhythm (and more), the fundamentals that make music sound good!

  • Chord progressions and harmony
  • Song structure and form
  • Songwriting basics
  • Keys, scales, intervals and much more
